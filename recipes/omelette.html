Omelette Recipe

The Perfect Omelette

Wondering how to make an omelette that’s pillowy, perfectly folded, and has just the right amount of filling? Well, trust me when I say this is a simple, no-fuss method that works every time. And if you need some filling ideas – I’ve got several delicious options that’ll please breakfast enthusiasts.

Ingredient List

  • 1 tbs oil, to coat pan
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2oz cheese filling

Steps

  1. Saute the filling. Before you actually make the omelette, you’ll need to saute your choice of filling (I’ve listed out options below). Then set the filling aside on a separate plate.
  2. Make the egg base. Whisk the eggs, salt and pepper in a medium bowl until lightly fluffy. And if you’re wondering why you should salt beforehand – I’ve got a quick explanation down below!
  3. Cook the omelette base. The key to the perfect omelette is cooking low and slow! So heat the oil or butter over medium-low heat, then pour the egg mixture into an even layer. Turn the heat to low, and let it cook without poking or prodding for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Add in the good stuff. Pile the filling onto one side of the omelette. Then gently fold the other half over and slowly slide the omelette onto a plate. Breakfast is served!
